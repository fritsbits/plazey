## Summary: what we have vs. what we need

**Well covered:**
- Festival atmosphere / crowd shots (multiple options, different angles)
- Music performance (both close-up detail and wide stage shots)
- Kids activities (5 photos covering various workshop types)
- Food / eten & drinken (4 distinct food/bar contexts)
- Volunteer / Plazey t-shirt moments (4 photos)
- Park identity / Elisabethpark establishing shots (including the Basilica backdrop)

**Needs more:**
- **Evening/dusk atmosphere** — only 1 evening shot (`538980955`). A photo-centric site needs at least 2–3 more showing the festival after sunset (string lights, candlelit food stalls, evening crowd).
- **Toegankelijkheid** — meaningful photos showing the festival being physically and sensorially accessible: accessible paths, sign language interpreters, quiet zones. The ear-protection product shot alone is not enough.
- **Main stage from audience perspective** — no wide shot of a large audience facing a main stage. This is a classic festival visual that's missing from the set.
- **Behind the scenes / setup** — volunteer setup and preparation photos for the "Doe mee" page.
- **Organiser portraits** — a human face for GC De Platoo / GC De Zeyp and the people behind the festival.
- **Programme edge cases** — spoken word, film, or other non-music/non-workshop formats have no matching images.
- **Horizontal/landscape crops** — several strong photos are portrait-oriented (vertical). A photo-centric site's hero slots are typically wide landscape. `537787901`, `529568369`, and `503383143` are the best landscape options. More horizontal crowd/stage shots would help.
